Skills & Jobs

A group of venture capitalists heard about our Green Thumb Project, and were very impressed. That they want to fund us to develop it further within the timeframe of 6 months. There will be a manager of the team that has 4 other members. 

These are the position descriptions that are appropriate for our project to take it onto the next phase.

Data Scientist/Machine-Learning Engineer

Green Thumb is looking to hire the services of an experienced Data Scientist/Machine Learning Engineer. They will be familiar with using Machine Learning and AI tools to identify certain trends in data, specifically the trends in the measurements collected by Green Thumb probes. Additionally they are also responsible for collecting large amounts of raw and seemingly random strings of data and converting and curating them to a usable state, and using that data, provide appropriate steps in to craft and enhance the user experience. The successful applicant will have an innovative and creative mindset to efficiently construct solutions to organise data effectively, and remove any irrelevant data so it can be used with the Machine Learning system.

The successful candidate will show strong teamwork skills as it is likely that they will work in groups in order to achieve their tasks. They are expected to be able to present their analytical insights as appropriate responses and actions to clients, possible stakeholders and business executives. Ideally, the successful candidate will have some management and leadership qualities as they will be required to liaise and manage a team of horticulturalists who will be needed in order to obtain appropriate data to feed the Machine Learning systems.

Appropriate Skills Needed:

  • Analytical skills to understand and identify data trends.
  • Strong attention to detail as to effectively curate large samples of data.
  • Problems solving and critical thinking skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team.

Specific Technical Expertise:

  • Skill in using programming languages such as SAS, R, and Python.
  • Knowledge of SQL and databases
  • Knowledge of Data visualization and reporting technologies.
  • Experience in building machine learning models from the ground up
  • Masters degree in data science, information management, computer science (artificial intelligence) or relevant discipline
  • Experience using SAS Viya Machine or Amazon SageMaker
  • Experience using PyTorch or other machine learning libraries
Software Architect

Green Thumb is looking to employ a highly skilled Software Architect. This person will need to demonstrate a range of IT skills to plan and build our mobile software and also have an understanding of the technology being built by our C++ engineer and UX/UI specialist in order to collaborate to create an effective and efficient program.

Our software architect requires teamwork experience, as they will need to collaborate and work with other developers while working on the project. They also need to be able to liaise with our team to understand what they need to achieve in the development of their software. We are looking for someone who is an innovative thinker to design our software in a way that is not only user friendly but also sets us apart from other similar products on the market. Green Thumb combines a range of different technologies and ideas, therefore we need someone who is able to construct something that cohesively brings it all together.

Appropriate Skills Needed:

  • Analytic skills to understand and identify problems to be solved.
  • Time management skills to make sure project deadlines are met.
  • Strong attention to detail.
  • Problems solving and critical thinking skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team 

Specific Technical Expertise:

  • Ability to code in a range of relevant programming languages like C++, Java.
  • Knowledge of SQL and databases
  • HTML and CSS experience
  • Knowledge of UI/UX design
  • Degree in Computer Science or Software Engineering highly desirable  
C#/C++ Engineer

Green Thumb is looking for a Software Engineer that has experience in programming in the C#/C++ language. As a C#/C++ Engineer you will be responsible for designing, programming and implementing a circuit board to detect soil health for our exciting new project. Additionally, you will collaborate with our software architects to integrate the circuit with a mobile application. For this reason you will be required to consult with other Green Thumb team members, to understand the scope and aims of the project. As such we are looking for someone who has strong communication skills. You will also be testing, debugging and performing ongoing maintenance of the Green Thumb circuit and application.

The ideal candidate will be someone who is capable of innovative thinking, the position will require creativity and the ability to solve problems and troubleshoot when things do not work as originally intended.

Appropriate Skills Needed:

  • Analytical skills to understand and identify data trends.
  • Accute attention to detail.
  • Problems solving and trouble shooting skills.
  • Ability to work effectively in a team.
  • Organisation and time managment skills.

Specific Technical Expertise:

  • Fluency in C/C++
  • Proficiency using Arduino IDE and experience in creating circuits with Arduino technologies.
  • Preferably has a Bachelor of Software Development or similar qualification.
  • Preferably has knowledge about SQL, specifically mySQL or similar.
UX + UI Designer

Green Thumb is searching for a User Experience/User Interface (UX/UI) Designer. Green Thumb’s UX/UI designer will be responsible for designing and constantly improving the Green Thumb Applications’ Digital Interface. They will ensure that it remains a user friendly and responsive interface. The successful candidate will have strong teamwork skills including interpersonal and communication skills; as they will be required to assist in product design, researching and analysing data insights into design solutions and create an interface for it. They will also have to collaborate with clients to create an interface that is easy to understand and simple to use and improve over time.

The ideal candidate will show a mindset of creativity and innovation as Green Thumb’s UX/UI designers are required to create new ways of expressing the company’s icon, communicating its information towards its clients in an engaging manner.

Appropriate Skills Needed:

  • Skills in wireframing and prototyping
  • Competent in visual communication
  • Ability to collaborate with developers, clients and team members
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • 2-3 years experience in a similar role

Specific Technical Expertise:

  • Ability to code with HTML, Javascript and CSS
  • Experience in Adobe XD and Creative Suite or similar
  • Experience in developing UX/UI for multiple device platforms
  • Experience in design systems such as Google Material, Figma or similar
  • Experience with CRM (customer relationship management) tools
