Scopes and Limits

Green Thumb is a large project that requires a significant amount of development. Outlined below is the scope and limitations for developing Green Thumb within the assigned time frame.

Out of scope Limitations In Scope
Engineering the Green Thumb soil probe and having it ready for market We are not able to design our own parts from scratch and will be limited to using preconstructed/generic parts.This is due to our own lack of skills in the area of engineering and limitations of our budget. Build a prototype of the circuit using arduino and generic technology. The Prototype will be able to measure soil temperature, moisture and pH and will have the capability to be connected to a mobile device.
Developing and designing the Green Thumb app ready for market Limited experience in programming and creating applications. Time constraints and limited opportunity to test applications. We will design the user interface for the Green Thumb app and develop a prototype to demonstrate the app's functionalities.
Programming and securing the Green Thumb database to support the app and AI. Limited experience working with SQL and Databases, which is why we are unable to create a prototype. We will develop an Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) as a proof-of-concept. The ERD will identify the types of data stored and the relationships between them. Additionally, we will propose a Cyber Security Strategy to protect the database.
Deploying the Green Thumb AI featuring:
  • Chatbot
  • Machine learning model
  • Image recognition
  • Classifier
 Data science and AI are outside of our skills set, so we will not be able to deliver a prototype for the AI. We will research and develop an AI strategy to be implemented by a data scientist. This strategy will identify the best technologies and software to build the Green Thumb AI with guidance for the type of data to import and snippets of code to assist with the deployment of the AI.
