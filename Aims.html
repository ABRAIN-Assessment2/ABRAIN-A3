Aims

The overall goal of Green Thumb is to develop a mobile app that accurately tracks indoor plant health and has the ability to diagnose diseases using diagnostic tools including a soil probe, image recognition and AI. With the combined knowledge and expertise of skilled programmers and horticulturalists we hope to create a highly accurate diagnostic tool that can compete with similar products that are currently on the market. In order to achieve our main aim we have compiled a list of more specific goals that will combine to create the completed Green Thumb project.



Engineer the Green thumb soil probe
We need to engineer a soil probe that measures soil temperature, pH levels and moisture. The probe will be engineered based upon Arduino infrastructure and programmed to transmit soil health data to our app which will then be tracked and stored in our database. The probe will be remotely controlled from the phone app. The soil probe is the key to the success of Green Thumb as it provides our project with a competitive advantage over other similar products on the market. As such, we will prioritise the development of the probe throughout this project to ensure adequate resources and attention is allocated.
